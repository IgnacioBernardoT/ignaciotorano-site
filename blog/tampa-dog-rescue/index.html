Watch the Rescue

Davis Islands Dog Park · Tampa, FL · 2026

Before I became a real estate professional helping people buy and sell homes across Tampa, I was already deeply connected to the neighborhoods, parks, and everyday moments that make this city special.

One moment I will never forget happened at Davis Islands Dog Park in Tampa, Florida.

While at the park, a dog nearly drowned and stopped breathing. It was a scary situation — but thanks to the quick actions of bystanders and Tampa first responders, the dog was revived and given a second chance. I happened to capture part of the rescue on video, and the story was later picked up by several news outlets across the country.

What stood out to me most was not just the rescue itself — it was the way people came together. Neighbors, bystanders, and first responders all reacted without hesitation. It was one of those moments that reminds you how strong and caring the Tampa community really is.

Why This Story Matters to Me

Davis Islands is one of Tampa's most recognizable waterfront neighborhoods — known for its wide streets, bayfront parks, walkability, and tight-knit community feel. But moments like this reveal something even more meaningful than the real estate: the people who live here.
