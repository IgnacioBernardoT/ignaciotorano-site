1. Claim & Fully Complete Your Google Business Profile

This is the #1 thing for local SEO. When someone searches "South Tampa real estate agent" or "Tampa Bay realtor," Google shows local profiles first. You need to own this completely.

1
Go to business.google.com and claim ignaciotorano.com as your business
2
Set category to "Real Estate Agent" — add secondary: "Real Estate Agency"
3
Add ALL photos — headshot, office, listings, neighborhood photos
4
Write a keyword-rich description: "Ignacio Toraño is a South Tampa native and licensed Realtor serving buyers and sellers across Tampa Bay including Hyde Park, Davis Islands, Palma Ceia, and the greater South Tampa area..."
5
Add your website, phone, hours, and services
6
Ask every satisfied client for a Google review — this is massive for rankings
Critical — Do This Week

Your YouTube channel is a massive SEO asset most realtors ignore. Google owns YouTube — videos rank on page 1 of Google searches constantly.

1
Title every video with location keywords: "Hyde Park Tampa Neighborhood Tour 2025" not just "neighborhood tour"
2
First 3 lines of every description: include "South Tampa real estate agent," your phone number, and ignaciotorano.com
3
Add tags: South Tampa, Tampa Bay real estate, Hyde Park Tampa, Davis Islands, Tampa realtor, buy home Tampa
4
Post consistently — even once a week. Google rewards active channels.
5
Embed your YouTube videos on your website (the Videos page already does this!)

6. Google Review Strategy

Reviews are the #1 ranking factor for local SEO. Every single client should leave you a Google review.

1

2
Get your direct review link from your Google Business Profile dashboard and save it
3
Respond to every review — Google notices engagement
4
Target: 50+ reviews in year 1. This alone can put you on page 1 locally.
